Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from other IPTV services?

Most providers use a single, often overloaded, server. We operate a multi-network platform. This means we integrate several premium, independent IPTV services into one account for you. Our system automatically switches you to the best-performing stream, virtually eliminating buffering and downtime. You get the content and stability of 6+ services for the price of one.

What devices are supported?

Our service works on virtually any device, including Amazon Firestick, Android TV/Boxes, Smart TVs (Samsung, LG), Apple TV, iOS & Android phones/tablets, and computers. We provide an M3U link and Xtream Codes login that are compatible with hundreds of apps.

Can I use it on multiple devices at once?

Our standard plans are for one active stream at a time. However, you can add more connections to your plan for a small additional fee to watch on multiple devices simultaneously. Please contact our support team to customize your plan.
